Classification of 2-manifolds

Job Feldbrugge

Compact two-dimensional manifolds can be classified in terms of the genus and the orientability of the manifold. I here describe a beautiful topological proof using combinatorial surfaces.

Theorem: Every connected two-dimensional compact surface is either a sphere, a sphere with \(n\)-handles, or a sphere with \(k\) cross caps.
